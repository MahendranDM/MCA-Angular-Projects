Department Info

Computer Science and Engineering

Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is one of the most sought after departments in the field of engineering. It offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers various aspects of computer science, including programming, algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and more.

Information Management and Computer Applications

The IMCA department focuses on the integration of information technology and management principles. It equips students with the skills needed to manage information systems, analyze data, and make informed business decisions.

Master of Computer Applications

The MCA department offers advanced studies in computer applications, preparing students for careers in software development, database management, and IT consulting. The program emphasizes practical skills and industry-relevant knowledge.

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering department focuses on the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems. Students gain expertise in areas such as thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and materials science.

Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering department is dedicated to the planning, design, and construction of infrastructure projects. Students learn about structural engineering, transportation systems, and environmental sustainability.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The EEE department focuses on the study of electrical systems, electronics, and power generation. Students gain knowledge in areas such as circuit design, control systems, and renewable energy technologies.

Master of Business Administration

The MBA department offers a comprehensive program in business management, covering areas such as finance, marketing, human resources, and strategic planning. Students develop leadership skills and business acumen to excel in the corporate world.
